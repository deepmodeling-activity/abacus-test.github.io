1. Introduction
By performing symmetry analysis, we can effectively improve the computational efficiency and accuracy. In this test, we have used ABACUS to calculate a series of crystal configurations with and without considering symmetry, and compared their energy differences, maximum force deviations, and maximum stress deviations. We hope that this test can reflect the accuracy of the symmetry module in ABACUS. Click here to check more details of the calculations.
