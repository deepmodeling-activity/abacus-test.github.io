1. Introduction
This test used ABACUS to calculate the SCF of a series of systems with symmetry set to 1 and 0, and compared the differences in energy/force/stress between them. The datasets include different point groups and space groups. We have conducted the same testing using VASP (job link) and QE (job link), and based on the results, we have established the passing criteria for this test. The criteria of force and stress is applied for each component. The pass ratio is the number of systems that meet the criteria divided by the total number of systems.
