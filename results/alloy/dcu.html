1. Introduction
This test utilized ABACUS to perform the SCF calculations on a series of alloy systems (structures are downloaded from material project) containing various elements performed on CPU device and DCU device. Both calculations use the plane wave basis and use ks_solver dav. The calculations are performed on Sugon platform with node kshcnormal (CPU) and kshdnormal (DCU).
