1. Introduction
The bandgap is a key parameter for semiconductor systems, and in this test, we employed the ABACUS package in conjunction with the HSE hybrid functional to calculate the bandgaps of a series of typical semiconductors. Furthermore, we compared the results between abacus and reference HSE and experimental values. The reference values are obtained from the following sources: 10.1021/acs.jpclett.8b00919 and 10.1021/acs.jpclett.0c00481. Click here to check the ABACUS inputs.
